Sorry for late reply, we usually go with these types of questions on discord, it's our "chat room", you don't have to beg for anything, don't make it sound like it's a huge deal to just hop on discord and tag any of open.mp developers you want to ask your questions, people usually don't check Burgershot, there's no tag system, there's no notification system, there's no way to be notified about a new thread posted and asking about something specific that me or any open.mp dev should hop on and answer.
