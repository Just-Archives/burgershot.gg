Southland Roleplay - Heavy RP - Ballas - 2021-10-14

Southland Roleplay is an upcoming roleplay server on SAMP coming out in mid January!

It's the passion project of players from several well-known heavy roleplay servers who want to keep the English roleplay scene alive on SAMP. It features one of the most advanced scripts that have SAMP as ever seen with a custom overlay, graffiti system, in-game internet, and phone system. There has truly never been anything like Southland. This discord has over 1,000 members and could be SAMP's last hope for English roleplay.

Most importantly it has a staff team that will listen, they've seen the downfall of the other English servers over the years and know the community drives a roleplay server.
