RE: The progress of Open.mp is great? - iAmir - 2021-12-29

Sorry for late reply, we usually go with these types of questions on discord, it's our "chat room", you don't have to beg for anything, don't make it sound like it's a huge deal to just hop on discord and tag any of open.mp developers you want to ask your questions, people usually don't check Burgershot, there's no tag system, there's no notification system, there's no way to be notified about a new thread posted and asking about something specific that me or any open.mp dev should hop on and answer.

Anyway, going back on topic, June 2021 we started a fresh rewrite, starting from ground up, basically threw old open.mp code away, and now we are finished, everything is done and fully compatible with samp scripts, running smoothly, currently being tested by a few using their gamemodes, to find some major issues first then we go on a semi-public beta testing phase, expect some bigger news from us as well, which will be posted, again, on our discord's announcement channel, which is probably the best place to inform everyone in it, not the few who are *also* using forums alongside discord.
