Lawless Roleplay [0.3.7 | EN] - Lui - 2022-02-07

Lawless Roleplay is a reasonably old server that opened it was doors back in 2013. In that short space of time, we have set the golden standard for any SA-MP community. When you join Lawless Roleplay, you enter into the city of Los Santos, a city infested with corruption, crime, and lunatics. Your role in the city will depend on the actions you take. What you do determines your value in Los Santos. Rise above the law or protect it, the choice is yours!
We pride ourselves in excellent administrators, staff, and features, but most importantly, fantastic players. We have been able to foster this great community by following strictly to our mission statement.
Lawless Roleplay is, and always, will be a community first and a game server second. We are dedicated to fostering an environment with a member-centric approach where all players have equal potential for in-game success and community recognition.
Lawless Roleplay was initially founded on the principle that all players should rise or decline based on the actions of the person behind the player. We feel that success should be determined by a players' actions and talents and not the whim of an administrative team. We have great code. We have great staff. We have great players. But most importantly, we have a vigilant regard for your potential.
We have worked to provide many in-game tools to encourage roleplay, however roleplaying is not required to play here. We feel that any preferred mode of play that does not infringe on any other player’s enjoyment of the game is productive. Due to the harsh nature of the game mode, frequent player versus player (PvP), violent interaction should be expected. However, this does not mean that players are allowed to kill other players for no reason.
