The idea is very good, however, I think you should have a form of registration by the application itself that would automatically register in the forum and other platform tools.

As it is done on steam, the person has an account with registered, confirmed email, has a system of achievement, a system of gifts, as it should be done on the servers of open.mp, something really different, that the players had a profile on open.mp whose administration of a server can enter and see the ban history of that player, etc ... It would be wonderful to manage this for servers with WhiteList or servers that suffer from attacks by malicious players, not to mention options that can be done as:

Server configuration:
- Only accept profiles with more than 100 hours played.
- Accept only profiles with good reputation from the players.
- Prohibit players with a high level of complaints from registering on the server.

Server system approved in OPEN.MP:
- A system that can be done paid and / or on merit.
- Those who pass will be at the top
- It will have the game mode that the server operates.
- Achievements of the server (approved the achievements of the server by the OPEN.MP team and the player will have a panel of achievements with an image of the achievement and on which server it was, to be displayed to all people who visit that player's profile)

I have a lot more idea in my head, but the text would be immense, not to mention that I have very bad English because I am Brazilian.
