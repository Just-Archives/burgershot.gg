0.3DL was supposed to be the update that prolonged sa-mps lifespan. It was supposed to give a fresh gust of wind. I think everyone wouldn't mind that being in their multiplayer mode. The reason why you are asking if the custom models are needed is because it's just the start. You have so much work to do and you can't do everything right now. You would not be asking this same question if you had finished polishing everything up and had nothing to worry about. You would just do it (not advertising Nike here).

We all know through sa-mp experience that the majority of the servers didn't do very well with exploiting the capabilities that sa-mp had available. But some did pretty amazing works. The majority and minority thing is everywhere. You just have to not mind that. In my mind the minority evens things out or in some cases even outweighs and makes you have a different mindset.

Modeling is also not something you can learn in a week. On top of that creating things takes time also. So I didn't expect sa-mp servers to be pulling things out of the magician hat right away.

Having this wouldn't make things worse. On the contrary. But as I said. There is no hurry. Better focus on more important things and have a healthy start.
