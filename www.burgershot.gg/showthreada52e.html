We are not planning on adding any add-ons to the GTA:SA World by default (excluding custom objects)

However we plan to make it possible for server developers to add these kind of things themselves. It wouldn't make any sense for us to do this. Just because a large amount of Russians plan to use our mod doesn't mean we will only support your region of the world. We will try and support everyone equally, with the same quality and assurances.

Do remember that at some point this mod will be open sourced, and that gives you the freedom to create your own version of the mod with lets say `Russian Maps` (if you wanted to go that way).
